# Polished Warboots

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/Polished Warboots.gif>)

Polished Warboots are diamond boots with 14 enchantments: 6 vanilla enchants and 8 AE enchants. It has 2 more enchants than its predecessor, Tantrum Greaves.
