# Pristine Sabatons

![](<../../.gitbook/assets/Pristine Sabatons.gif>)

Pristine Sabatons are iron boots with 17 enchantments: 6 vanilla enchants and 11 AE enchants. It has 3 more enchants than its predecessor, Polished Warboots.
